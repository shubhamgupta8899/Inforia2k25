FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Inforia?
Inforia is a fest that is conducted annually in IES University and IES College of Technology where you can participate in many events including technical, non-technical, Sports and cultural.
How can I participate in Inforia?
You can participate by contacting Inforia coordinators or by filling out the form by clicking here.
What are the perks of participating in Inforia?
Inforia provides platform for skill development, networking opportunities, problem-solving challenges, and exposure to showcase your talent within dynamic professional environments and a certificate by college.
